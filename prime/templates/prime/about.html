Our Mission

Sharpening minds,
one question at a time.

Prime Point was founded on a simple belief: the best way to build a strong mind is through deliberate, structured practice. We connect passionate learners with expert masters and give them the tools to grow — at their own pace, on their own terms.

We are not just a quiz app. We are a full learning ecosystem — from homework assignments to live discussion, from beginner practices to expert-level challenges.

Deliberate Practice Targeted, measurable improvement every session
Expert Mentorship Learn directly from masters in their field
Visible Progress Track your rank from Novice to Grandmaster
Community Learning Discuss, debate, and grow alongside peers
What We Offer

Everything you need to level up.

Practice Hub

Hundreds of curated practice sessions across subjects and difficulty levels. Filter by topic, master, or level — and track your score every attempt.

Homework System

Masters assign targeted practice as homework to individual students or groups. Deadlines, auto-grading, and submission tracking built in.

Master Profiles

Follow experts in your field. Each master curates their own practice library and can guide their students through structured learning paths.

Discussion Forum

Engage with the community — ask questions, share insights, vote on answers, and learn from conversations that go beyond the practice sheet.

Rank System

Earn points through practice and climb from Novice to Grandmaster. Your rank reflects your dedication — and motivates you to keep going.

Analytics

Masters see exactly where each student struggles — question by question, choice by choice. Targeted feedback becomes effortless.
